Donut recipe

Homemade Donuts

Indulge in the ultimate homemade donut experience with our easy-to-follow recipe. These donuts are perfectly soft, fluffy, and golden brown, offering a delightful treat for any occasion. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe ensures delicious results every time.

With just a few simple ingredients and steps, you can create a batch of mouthwatering donuts that are perfect for breakfast, a snack, or even dessert. Enjoy them plain or get creative with your favorite toppings and glazes to make each donut uniquely yours.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or canola)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for coating)
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon (for coating)

Steps

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, combine 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1 tablespoon baking powder, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together 1/2 cup whole milk, 2 large eggs, 1/4 cup melted unsalted butter, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  3. Combine: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Roll and Cut: On a floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/2-inch thickness. Use a donut cutter to cut out the donuts.
  5. Heat Oil: In a large pot, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry Donuts: Carefully place the donuts in the hot oil and fry until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Coat: In a shallow dish, mix 1 cup granulated sugar with 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon. Roll the warm donuts in the mixture to coat.
  8. Serve: Enjoy your homemade donuts warm or at room temperature.
